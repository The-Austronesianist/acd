2262

*iri {??} {?? really a subentry?}

POC

OC
Lonwolwol iryonder, over there
Nggela iridemonstrative: those

Also ISG iddé 'this, here', MALOH indiʔ 'this, these', ACH hideh 'yonder, over there', OJ iri 'particle with prepositional function = í, TAE' inde 'this, here'. This item, ultimately analyzable into *i 'generic marker of location' + *di, apparently was a third person demonstrative, in contrast to *itu. Under this interpretation, however, reflexes meaning 'this; here' in Isneg, Tagalog, and Moken remain puzzling.
