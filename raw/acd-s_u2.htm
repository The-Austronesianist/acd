*úlit repeat

6074

PPh     *úlit repeat
WMP
Ilokano úlitrepeat, reiterate
Pangasinan ólitrepeat; again
Kapampangan úlitrepeat
Tagalog úlitrepetition, renewal of action
Bikol ulítrepeat something, do something again, redo
Bolaang Mongondow (g)ulitrestore something to its original condition
Gorontalo wulitorepeat, mention again
Dampelas ulitrepeat
Totoli ulitrepeat

This item shares many of the same semantic characteristics as Proto-Malayo-Polynesian *uliq₁ (Proto-Philippine *ulíq). The primary sense of *uliq appears to have been 'return, restore', from which 'repeat' is derived. The primary sense of *ulit, on the other hand, appears to have been 'repeat', from which the notions of renewal and restoration are derived in turn.
