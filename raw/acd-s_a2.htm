*ábuR₁ chase, drive away

6053

PPh     *ábuR₁ chase, drive away
WMP
Isneg ma-ábugbe driven away
Bontok ábolchase away chickens or animals, using a stick; the stick so used
Kankanay ábudrive away, turn out, expel
Ifugao ábulchase, repel, drive away
Batad Ifugao ābulfrighten away chickens, rice birds, pigs, flies in any effective manner
Ilokano ábugdrive, drive away, incite
Cebuano ábugchase, drive, drive away
Mansaka abogdrive away, chase away
Dibabawon Manobo abugdrive away, drive out, chase
Western Bukidnon Manobo avugto drive, as animals; drive away, force out
Maranao abogscatter, drive away

Also Tagalog abóy 'something or someone being driven about; act of driving away' (probably an early Kapampangan loan). The similarity of Yamdena yabur 'flee, run away' to the forms cited above is assumed to be a product of chance.

6089

PPh     *átaŋ átaŋ poisonous arthropod
WMP
Casiguran Dumagat átaŋ-átaŋspecies of poisonous river crab
Tagalog átaŋ-átaŋscorpion

Possibly a Tagalog loan in Casiguran Dumagat, although both the discrepancy in meaning between the forms, and the use of the Casiguran Dumagat term to refer to a natural organism make this unlikely.
